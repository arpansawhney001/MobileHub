Who are we?

We are a small group of developers who met through the 2018 Grow with Google Challenge. This mulit month long online educational series was created by Udacity and sponsored by Google.

We are all located in Raleigh, North Carolina and were placed in the same Mobile Web Specialist program. Soon, we teamed up to help each other by sharing useful tips and resources that would help us grow technically and professionally. And now we'd like to extend the same resources to you.
